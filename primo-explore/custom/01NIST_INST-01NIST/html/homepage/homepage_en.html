The NIST Library Search provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

It also helps you manage your research. NIST staff, you can sign in to:

  • Manage your “Library Account”
  • Save item records to your “Favorites”
  • Save searches to your “Favorites”
  • Export item records to other applications
  • Place holds on physical items
  • Renew items you have checked out
 What's New with Library Search?

The November update includes the following key enhancements:

  • Person Info Card
  • This "What's New" card on the landing page
  • Carousel, featuring NIST Authors in Journal of Visualized Experiments (JoVE)
  • Identifiers now display in each record, e.g., DOI, ISBN, OCLC Number

Additional details on current and past releases can be found on https://github.com/usnistgov/primo-explore-devenv/releases
